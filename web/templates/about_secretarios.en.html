About the state secretaries datasets

The Federative Research Network ("Rede Federativa de Pesquisadores") was created with the objective of building an unprecedented database that gathers information on the high position officers in state and Federal District governments: the state secretaries.

The Network focused on basic information on state secretaries between 1995 and 2010. Data was mainly collected by local researchers (from each state or Federal District) that was participating on the Network.

The main sources for data colletction were the official gazettes, once they are the official means of communication of the state governments in Brazil. Data on party affiliation and political candidatures was collected from the Brazilian Superior Electoral Court data repositories.

The publication of this dataset makes the analysis of the 26 Brazilian states' and Federal District's secretaries possible for the above mentioned period. Political and personal characteristics of secretaries are available. The uniformity of the information format was guarateed through the use of an online plataform by the research team to enter the collected data: http://ipe.cepesp.io/

We continue to work updating data for recent period, until 2018.

This project was developed with financial support from the São Paulo State Foundation for Research (FAPESP) to the projects Political Institutions and Public Spending: A Comparative Study of the Brazilian States (2009-2013) and The Subnational Political Institutions: A Comparative Study of the Brazilian States (2014-2020). This project was also supported by Fundação Getúlio Vargas through the project Visualizing politics .
